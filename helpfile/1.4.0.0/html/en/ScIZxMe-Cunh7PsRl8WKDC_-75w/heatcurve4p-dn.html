HeatCurve4P_DN (FB)¶

FUNCTION_BLOCK HeatCurve4P_DN

Short Description¶

Heating curve with four outdoor temperature points and two characteristic curves (night, day).

Functional Description¶


The supply setpoint temperature rSupplyTemp is dependent on the current outside temperature rOutTemp
and the selected characteristic curve (night characteristic curve: xDay = FALSE // day characteristic curve: xDay = TRUE).
The supply setpoint temperature rSupplyTemp is limited by adjustable absolute minimum (rSupplyTempMin) and
the maximum values ​​(rSupplyTempMax).

The characteristic curves are defined and adjusted respectively by four outdoor temperature points (separated for the night and day characteristic curve).
Each point is defined by an outside temperature and an allocated supply setpoint
temperatur. The four outside temperature points of a characteristic curve are connected by straight lines.

Limitation

rTo4 > rTo3 > rTo2 > rTo1
The parameter eManModeN allows the supply setpoint temperature rSupplyTemp to be switched from automatic to
manual operation and vice versa. In manual mode, the supply setpoint temperature rSupplyTemp assumes the value of
the parameter rManValue.
